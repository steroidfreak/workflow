Workflow SG WhatsApp Business Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 June 2024

Privacy Policy for Workflow SG's WhatsApp Experiences

This Privacy Policy describes how Workflow SG Pte. Ltd. ("Workflow SG", "we", "us" or "our") collects, uses, and protects personal data when organisations connect their WhatsApp Business accounts to Workflow SG products. It also explains the choices available to administrators, agents, and end-users who interact with these messaging services.

1. Scope & relationship with Meta

Workflow SG provides workflow automation and customer engagement tooling used by Singapore-based teams. When you authorise our integration inside Meta's Business Manager, you instruct Workflow SG to process WhatsApp Business Account data on your behalf. Meta remains the primary data controller for WhatsApp, while Workflow SG acts as your data intermediary and processor to deliver the requested services.

2. Information we collect

Depending on how you configure the integration, we may process:

  • Account identifiers such as WhatsApp Business Account IDs, phone numbers, display names, and verification status provided via Meta APIs.
  • Conversation records including opt-ins, message templates, delivery receipts, media metadata, and agent notes that flow through your workspace.
  • Operational telemetry like integration health signals, automation triggers, escalation events, and API error codes generated by our platform.
  • Administrative details such as the names, emails, and roles of administrators configuring the integration within Workflow SG.

We do not require or request end-user account credentials for Meta or WhatsApp. Sensitive information contained in message payloads remains encrypted in transit and at rest as described below.

3. How we use WhatsApp data

Workflow SG processes WhatsApp data solely to:

  • Deliver and orchestrate conversations that your organisation initiates or receives.
  • Provide analytics dashboards, audit trails, and automation playbooks requested by your team.
  • Monitor service quality, detect abuse, and maintain platform security.
  • Comply with applicable laws, regulatory requirements, and Meta platform policies.

We do not sell WhatsApp data or use it to build advertising profiles. Data from WhatsApp is segregated from other Workflow SG customer environments and is not combined for cross-customer analytics without explicit consent.

4. Sharing & disclosure

Workflow SG does not disclose WhatsApp data to third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers: vetted infrastructure partners (such as hosting, security monitoring, and support tooling) who process data under contractual confidentiality and data protection obligations.
  • Legal requirements: when necessary to comply with court orders, regulatory requests, or to enforce our Terms of Service. We will notify the customer unless legally prohibited.
  • Customer direction: when administrators export data, enable connectors, or grant access to additional parties within their organisation.

5. Data retention

WhatsApp conversation records and associated automation logs are retained for the duration of your active contract and may be configured for shorter retention in accordance with your compliance policies. When a customer disables the integration or requests deletion, Workflow SG deletes or anonymises WhatsApp data within 30 days unless a longer period is required by law.

6. Security safeguards

Workflow SG implements administrative, technical, and physical safeguards aligned with ISO 27001 and Singapore PDPA expectations, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest.
  • Role-based access controls with just-in-time elevation for support engineers.
  • Continuous monitoring, intrusion detection, and audit logging.
  • Annual penetration testing and vulnerability remediation.

7. International data transfers

Workflow SG hosts production services in Singapore. If support or redundancy requires a transfer outside Singapore, we rely on contractual safeguards and only work with partners that maintain comparable data protection standards.

8. Your choices & rights

Customers may access, correct, export, or delete WhatsApp data at any time via the Workflow SG admin console or by contacting our support desk. End-users who wish to exercise PDPA or GDPR rights should first contact the organisation that sent them messages; we will support our customer in addressing the request.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect product enhancements or regulatory changes. Material updates will be communicated to administrators via email and in-product notices at least 14 days before the new terms take effect.
